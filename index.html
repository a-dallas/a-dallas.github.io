About Me

Hello! I'm currently a Master's student at Stanford, studying Management Science & Engineering. Previously, I worked in the aerospace industry for three years on a wide variety of projects, from commercial and defense, and manned to unmanned. For a complete resume, please check my LinkedIn.

I have a broad interest in technology, particularly in the application of computational technology and math to solve problems in the physical world. These interests span developing design and analysis tools (CFD is one area of note I've worked in), controllers and learning agents, and data analysis of macro trends. I'm also interested in technology strategy and management, and I'm always looking for opportunities where new technologies can be deployed to change the way the world works.
